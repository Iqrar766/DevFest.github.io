Event Overview

GDG Malakand brings you the most anticipated event of the year - DevFest Malakand 2023. A flagship event bringing together developers, thinkers and leaders from across the globe to connect, we focus on community building and Google's technologies. Join us for Devfest 2023 packed with learning, networking, experimenting and exclusive Google swags.

DevFest is an annual decentralized tech conference hosted by the Google Developer Groups (GDG) community. GDGs host these events around the globe. DevFest 2022 marks the 11th year of DevFest and represents the beginning of the second decade of the GDG community.
